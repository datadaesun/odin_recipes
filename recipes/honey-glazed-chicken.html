Honey Glazed Chicken Breast

Description

I received this recipe from a friend of mine and have used this for years. My family requests this one often. It's a light meal that everyone will enjoy. Serve with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes. Pour honey mixture into skillet and continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
